About Us

TranAssist was founded in 2017 by students of Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology who recognized the urgent need to advance transgender equality. With a committed team, we set out to accomplish what no one had yet done: provide a powerful transgender advocacy presence in India. Following is a list of our great CEOs who have made this possible.




Rishabh Sobti, as his followers describe him, is "a complete package of skills". He is the head of the Management Committee, and the Coder's Association of our company. Coding is his love. He eats code, he breathes code. Apart from that, he loves to travel, read books and meet new people. His organisation and management skills are beyond imagination, and his looks kill a thousand people everyday.







Tushar Chopra is the go-to guy, who knows how to get work done. He heads the HR branch and is the Internal Affairs Manager of our company. His pieces of code are a beauty, just like the shape of his beard. A gym freak, and coding expert. he literally lift dumbbells with one hand, and codes with the other. This guy is truly talented.








Advaya Shukla, is the one, who knows how to talk to people and handle situations. He is the head of Customer Relations, External Affairs and MUN. He provides his services, on demand, anytime, anywhere, with full enthusiasm. He is a true inspiration. He has always reminded us, that it is not only about code, it is 'About Us'.








Manu Dev is a very hard working and creative guy. He is the Managing Director and head of the Creative branch of the company. Manu is a fitness freak and armed forces aspirant. He knows how to get what he desires, through his hard work, will to learn and dedication. He is a true gem in our team of directors.






Shubham, along with his critical analysis skills and timing, is a very important part of this company. He heads the Testing and Maintenance Branch, and he is the one who makes sure that everything is going around smoothly in our corporate environment. He is a jolly good fellow, with dreams of becoming the undisputed king of the IT industry.
